# The quotient crater is a cubic over a cusp

The weighted symmetry of the Alpöge map reduces the three-dimensional crater
to an explicit polynomial map of affine planes. In the quotient, the same
three-sheeted geometry reappears as a second depressed cubic whose
discriminant separates an intrinsic rim from a projection shadow.

Machine verification: [`verify_quotient.py`](verify_quotient.py).

## Weighted quotient

The source and target weights are

```text
wt(x,y,z)=(-1,1,2),       wt(A,B,C)=(2,1,-1).
```

Every invariant source monomial satisfies `a=b+2c`, hence is
`(xy)^b(x^2z)^c`; similarly every invariant target monomial is generated by
`BC` and `AC^2`. Thus

```text
r=xy,  t=x^2z,            u=BC,  v=AC^2
C[x,y,z]^Gm = C[r,t],     C[A,B,C]^Gm = C[u,v].
```

The equivariant map descends to `Q:A^2_(r,t)->A^2_(u,v)`:

```text
u = -(3r+t-2)(9r^3+3r^2t+12r^2+6rt+r+3t),

v = (r+1)(3r+t-2)^2(3r^3+r^2t+4r^2+2rt+t).
```

This is the affine invariant quotient, not automatically a geometric orbit
space at unstable orbits. On `C!=0`, however, `(u,v,c=C)` is an actual
coordinate chart with inverse `A=v/c^2`, `B=u/c`.

## The rim is literally a cusp downstairs

Define the invariant forms

```text
ell = LC^2 = u^3-u^2-18uv+27v^2+16v,
s   = S    = 27v-9u+8.
```

Then

```text
s^2 = (4-3u)^3 + 27ell.
```

Consequently the quotient rim `ell=0` is the cuspidal cubic

```text
s^2=(4-3u)^3.
```

On the chart `C!=0`, the full rim is therefore `G_m` times this cusp. The
omitted floor orbit has `(u,v,s)=(4/3,4/27,0)` and collapses to the cusp tip.
This explains both the floor's orbit structure and the finite-field count
`q(q-1)+q=q^2` away from the two special characteristics; the separate
characteristic-2 and characteristic-3 arguments live in Discussion #29.

## Two nested depressed cubics

The original fiber equation

```text
Lx^3 + (4-3BC)x - 2C = 0
```

becomes monic after `X=Lx`, with

```text
P=(4-3BC)L,       Q=-2CL^2,
4P^3+27Q^2=4L^3S^2.
```

Thus its discriminant cusp is the universal depressed-cubic cusp.

Eliminating `t` from the quotient map produces another unexpectedly small
fiber equation. With `w=r+1`, a generic quotient fiber satisfies

```text
ell*w^3 + (u^2-12v)w - 4v = 0.
```

Set

```text
D = u^3-18uv+54v^2.
```

The corresponding square identity and discriminant are

```text
D^2 = (u^2-12v)^3 + 108ell*v^2,
disc_w = -4ell*D^2.
```

So quotienting does not erase the failure ecology. It reproduces it:

- `ell=0` is the intrinsic nonproperness rim, now visibly a cusp;
- `D=0` is the shadow where the invariant coordinate `r` stops separating
  sheets;
- away from `D=0`, the second invariant `t` is reconstructed rationally, with
  denominator `2D`.

The quotient map is generically cubic. Its trace form on `{1,r,r^2}` has

```text
det(T_Q) = -4D^2/ell^3.
```

Hence its determinant square class is `-ell`, exactly the descended intrinsic
class (`ell=LC^2` differs from `L` by a square on `C!=0`). The even `D^2`
factor is projection-dependent; the odd rim valuation survives. Over the
reals, the Sylvester-Hermite signature of this form counts real quotient
preimages, so the cusp `ell=0` is also the inertia wall.

## An exact monodromy loop

At the rational target `(-1/4,0,0)`, the three preimages are

```text
p0=(0,0,-1/4),
p+=(1,-3/2,13/2),
p-=(-1,3/2,13/2).
```

Along `lambda=e^(i theta)`, `0<=theta<=pi`, target equivariance gives the
closed loop

```text
(-lambda^2/4,0,0).
```

Its lifted action `(x,y,z)->(lambda^-1 x,lambda y,lambda^2 z)` fixes `p0` and
exchanges `p+` with `p-` at the endpoint. This is an exact symbolic
transposition around the rim, replacing a numerical continuation witness.

## Trace-form lesson

The original `x`-power lattice has determinant `-4S^2/L^3`. Changing to the
`y`-power lattice gives

```text
det(T_y) = -729 A^2 L / 4.
```

The `S^2` zero and the `L^-3` pole disappear, replaced by another even shadow
`A^2`; the odd class remains `-L`. The matrix and full divisor are therefore
basis-dependent, while the isometry class and determinant modulo squares are
intrinsic. The quotient trace form above gives a genuinely invariant-coordinate
realization rather than merely rescaling a non-invariant coordinate basis.
